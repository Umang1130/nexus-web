THE DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERING AND DATA SCIENCE

The Nexus Forum of the CSE-Data Science Department is a dynamic platform created by the students for the students to foster collaboration, knowledge sharing, and innovation among students, faculty, and industry experts.

Our Vision & Mission

Institute

Vision

To create a knowledge based society with clarity of thoughts and charity at hearts to serve humanity with integrity.

Mission

To empower youth to be technocrats of tomorrow with absolute discipline, quest for knowledge and strong ethos to uphold the spirit of professionalism.

Department

Vision

To develop data Science engineers to administer the data centric world for strengthening the modern society with analytical practices and research.

Mission

To upraise skilled data science professionals by imparting domain specific knowledge to contribute towards analytical data computing area with strong professional ethics and wide industry exposure.

Nexus Forum

Vision

To foster a dynamic platform where different minds meet to create something incredible through collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing.

Mission

To create a student-driven community that promotes learning, networking, and professional development through events, workshops, and collaborative projects.
